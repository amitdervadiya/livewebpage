About NYATI Safari

Luxurious Nyati Safari Lodge offers a unique African safari experience. At the lodge you can pamper yourself with first class gastronomy and a fine selection of South African wines, beers and all other beverages.

The lodge came to life back in 1998 and is 100% Danish owned. However, in order to ensure your truly authentic South African experience the lodge is run on a daily basis by a South African family and their choice of staff.

Focus on unique experiences:

  • Nyati Safari Lodge is situated on 10,000 hectares privately owned wilderness with direct access to the Greater Kruger National Park in South Africa.
  • You always have very good chances of spotting the “Big Five”. “Big 5” is a traditional hunter’s way of describing lion, leopards, elephants, rhinos and buffaloes.
  • When staying at Nyati Safari Lodge 2 daily game drives are included in your stay (depending on your arrival and departure times.)
  • Along the way you will have ample opportunity to spot the “Big 5” and the natural South African wildlife. And tomorrow morning? A new game drive awaits you.

Our Story

Born from a passion for
the untamed beauty of Africa

Our safari journey began over 10 years ago. What started as a dream to share the wonders of the savannah with the world has grown into a mission to provide unforgettable wildlife experiences.

Our journey began with a vision to redefine the safari experience by blending adventure with unparalleled luxury. For 10 years, we have been crafting bespoke safaris, combining the thrill of wildlife encounters with exquisite accommodations and world-class service. Every guest becomes part of our extended safari family, and every journey is crafted with heart, passion, and expertise.

We have been dedicated to curating world-class safari experiences across Africa’s most breathtaking landscapes. Our team of expert guides, conservationists, and hospitality professionals work together to ensure every experience is immersive, responsible, and unforgettable. As stewards of the land, we are deeply committed to wildlife conservation and sustainable tourism, partnering with local communities to protect Africa’s natural heritage.

Unique location

Not only is Nyati Safari Lodge situated with direct access to Greater Kruger National Park, but all accommodation types are also facing Olifant River. Olifant River never dries out, no matter how dry the summer gets, and the wildlife knows this.

No matter which type of accommodation you choose at Nyati, you will always have your own private picture terrace. This means that you can spot wildlife while sipping a drink on your terrace. Not bad, right?

For Families and Groups

You will feel at home at Nyati Safari Lodge, no matter if you are travelling alone or as a group. The lodge offers more types of accommodation, and for 1-4 people we recommend a closer look at either the River Bungalow, Olifant Villa Suite, the Rhino Tent or maybe the Family Bungalow is suitable for you.

In July 2005 Nyati Safari expanded the lodge with Cheetah Camp, a sumptuous independent lodge located on the Nyati grounds. The camp is perfect for groups up to 14 people and is especially suited to families with children. Cheetah Camp is the natural choice for families, groups of friends or clubs, who in the privacy of their own company can celebrate important birthdays, special occasions or have their stay adapted to their own special requests.

While staying at Cheetah Camp you will have a private cook and ranger at your service.

The breakfast table at Nyati Safari Lodge

Affordable luxury & delicious food

The Nyati Safari Lodge an all-round affordable luxury experience. The surroundings are unique, accommodation top-of-the-range, excursions and activities offer adventure and the culinary aspects are an experience in their own right.

The wide range of optional activities cater for every individual interests and offer our guests the ability to set the pace of the holiday themselves. We have an enthusiastic team of cooks who will serve supreme meals of international and South African national dishes and specialities.

For lunch and dinner you will have a starter, and then for the main course a selection of fish, meat or vegetarian dishes followed by a delicious dessert. Choose to complement your meals by a wide selection of the best South African wines, beer, juices or soda. The choice is yours and you can drink whatever you wish. All is included at Nyati Safari Lodge.
