Why Choose Us

Nyati Safari Lodge is situated on 10,000 hectares privately owned wilderness with direct access to the Greater Kruger National Park in South Africa. When staying at Nyati Safari Lodge 2 daily game drives are included in your stay (depending on your arrival and departure times).

About our story

Luxurious Nyati Safari Lodge offers a unique African safari
experience. At the lodge you can pamper yourself with first class
gastronomy and a fine selection of South African wine, beers
and other beverages. The lodge came to life back in
1998 and is 100% Danish owned.
