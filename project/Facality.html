Swimming Pool Safari Lodge

NYATI Safari Lodge offer a swimming pool for all guests.
Enjoy a dip in our spacious pool after an excellent Game Drive. The pool is open for all guests daily from 09:00 – 22:00
The swimming pool is heated when cold, in order to be enjoyed year round.
Are you staying at Olifant Villa Suites or at Cheetah Camp you will have your own private pool, available exclusively to you. Both pools are heated when cold just like the pool close to the main lodge.
